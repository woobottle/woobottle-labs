What should you do if you miss a supplement?

Short answer: If you miss a supplement, the usual approach is to take it when you remember if it's still the same day, or simply skip it and continue as normal — rather than doubling up to catch up. For medicines specifically, follow the label or ask your pharmacist, since rules can differ.

Don't double up to catch up

A single missed day of a supplement is rarely a big deal, and taking two at once to compensate is generally not the answer. In most cases, taking it when you remember (if it is still the same day) or skipping to the next scheduled time is enough.

Medicines can have different rules than supplements, so for anything prescribed, check the label or ask your pharmacist rather than guessing.

Frequently asked questions

Can I take two the next day to make up for it?

Generally no — doubling up isn't recommended for most supplements. Just resume your normal schedule.

Does missing one day undo my progress?

For most supplements the benefit comes from long-term consistency, so a single miss is minor. Focus on getting back on track.
