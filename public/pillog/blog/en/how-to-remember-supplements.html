How can you remember to take your supplements every day?

Short answer: The most reliable way to remember supplements is to attach them to something you already do every day and to use a reminder you can act on immediately. Anchoring to a fixed meal, keeping them visible, and checking each dose off tend to work better than relying on memory alone.

Anchor it to an existing habit

The easiest way to remember a new habit is to tie it to one you never skip — breakfast, brushing your teeth, or making coffee. When the trigger already happens daily, the supplement rides along with it.

Keeping the bottle visible where that habit happens gives you a physical cue, so you are not depending on memory in a busy morning.

Use a reminder you can act on right away

A notification only helps if you can respond to it in the moment. Pillog sends a reminder at your dose time, lets you check it off from the lock screen, and shows your month as a single card so you can see your streak building. Everything stays on your phone, with no account and no ads.

If you take several supplements, grouping the ones you take together into a single reminder keeps things simple and reduces the chance of missing one.

Frequently asked questions

What if I miss a dose?

Don't try to double up to make up for it. Just take the next one as usual, and consider whether your timing needs adjusting so it fits your day better.

Is it better to take supplements all at once?

Grouping ones that can be taken together makes the habit easier, but some are better spaced out. Your pharmacist can tell you which to separate.
