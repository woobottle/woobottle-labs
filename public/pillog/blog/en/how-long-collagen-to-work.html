How long does collagen take to work?

Short answer: Most people who try collagen give it a stretch of consistent daily use — often several weeks to a few months — before judging whether they notice anything. Results vary a lot from person to person, so steady daily habits tend to matter more than the exact time you take it.

Why patience and consistency matter

Collagen is usually taken daily over an extended period, and people's experiences differ widely. Because any change tends to be gradual, taking it sporadically makes it hard to tell whether it does anything for you.

Picking a fixed daily time — with a morning coffee or an evening drink — makes the habit easier to keep for the weeks it may take.
