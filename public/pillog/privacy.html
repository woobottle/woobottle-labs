1. Overview

Pillog ("the App") helps people who take multiple medications daily avoid missing doses. The App runs without any server or backend, and all information you enter is stored solely on your own device. The developer (woobottle) cannot access any of your personal information.

2. Information Stored

The App stores the following only in your device's local storage. This information never leaves your device.

ItemDetailsRequired
Medication infoName, dose, schedule, frequencyWhen used
Intake recordsDaily completion status and time takenAutomatic
NicknameUsed in the greetingOptional
App settingsNotification on/off, with-meal flagOptional

The App does not collect identity data, contacts, location, access to contacts/photos/camera/microphone, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics.

LEGAL REVIEW Medication data is health-related and may be sensitive under Korea's PIPA and a special category under GDPR Art.9, but the App keeps it on-device only and never transmits or collects it, so no processing by the developer occurs.

3. How Information Is Used

On-device data is used only on the device to: show local notifications at scheduled times; calculate today's schedule, progress, and streaks; and visualize history in the monthly calendar and weekly report.

4. Sharing, Processors, Transfers

None. The App transmits no data externally, so there is no third-party sharing, processors, or cross-border transfer. It contains no analytics, ad SDKs, or trackers. Notifications use the device OS's local notification facility; their content does not leave the device.

5. Retention and Deletion

Data is retained on your device until you delete it or remove the App. You can delete individual medications or records in-app. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data it stored. The developer keeps no backups.

6. Your Rights

Since all data resides on your device, you can access, correct, and delete it yourself at any time.

LEGAL REVIEW GDPR (EU) and CCPA/CPRA (California) may require specific rights wording depending on distribution regions.

7. Security

Data is stored in your device's local storage and protected by OS-level security (lock screen, app sandbox). No system is 100% secure; protecting the device against loss, theft, or malware is the user's responsibility.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 14 and transmits no information to the developer, so it does not collect children's personal information.

Updates will be posted here and on the store listing with a revised effective date; material changes may be communicated in-app.
